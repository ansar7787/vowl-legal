PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 2026

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, such as your name, email, and profile picture. We also collect data about your progress, quest completion, voice inputs for AI analysis, and interaction with the learning content.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information to personalize your language learning experience, track your quest progress, manage your gamification rewards (coins/XP), and improve our AI tutors to provide better feedback on your English skills.

3. ADVERTISING AND ANALYTICS

Our app includes optional third-party advertisements (e.g., rewarded ads to triple coins). These third-party ad networks may collect device identifiers and usage data to provide relevant ads. If you subscribe to Vowl Premium, ads are removed and this tracking is disabled.

4. IN-APP PURCHASES & SUBSCRIPTIONS

If you purchase Vowl Premium, your payment is processed securely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your credit card information on our servers.

5. DATA SECURITY & THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use Firebase (Google) for secure authentication and cloud data storage. We implement industry-standard security measures, including Firestore security rules, to protect your personal information.

6. ACCOUNT AND DATA DELETION

You have the right to access, update, or completely delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do this directly inside the app by going to Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account. Upon requesting deletion, all your personal data is permanently wiped from our databases.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (KIDS ZONE)

Vowl includes a dedicated "Kids Zone." We are strictly committed to protecting the privacy of children under 13. We do not serve targeted advertising in the Kids Zone, and we comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

8. CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support.vowl.app@gmail.com.
